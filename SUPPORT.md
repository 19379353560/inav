## Useful Support Topics

- Flight-test comparisons for `dterm_lpf2_hz` disabled, 200Hz, and 250Hz.
- Blackbox logs from noisy frames where D-term behavior is easy to compare.
- Documentation feedback on the setting name, default-disabled behavior, and
  conservative test procedure.
- Review of generated settings docs and filter-profile behavior.

For flight-test feedback, include firmware commit or PR build, target, aircraft
class, filter settings, Blackbox logs, and weather or vibration notes.
