# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes practical, technical feedback from INAV users, FPV
builders, and firmware reviewers.

## Expected

- Be specific and constructive.
- Include logs, firmware commits, configuration snippets, and reproduction notes
  when possible.
- Separate confirmed flight-test results from assumptions.
- Keep safety concerns clear and direct.

## Not Welcome

- Personal attacks, harassment, or insults.
- Unsafe flight advice presented as proven.
- Spam, fake engagement, or unrelated promotion.

Moderation decisions are made to keep review focused, safe, and useful.
